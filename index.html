Giving back to the commutnity

Boosting Sales: Onboarding a Momo Vendor on Zomato

Our neighborhood food cart seller was restricted to sales in the evenings. In spite of adequate capacity, sales were constrained by low footfalls and bad weather during daytime. I onboarded them onto Zomato (food delivery app) guiding him through documentation, creation of online menu complete with images, competitive pricing and required Certifications. I had to overcome their initial hesitation and fear of technology by persistent handholding and continued guidance. This resulted in increased sales by 15% within 20 days. Further improvement in sales has been planned by onboarding onto the second food delivery app-Swiggy and increasing their online presence.

From Notebook to Smartphone: Empowering a Vendor with Digital Tools

Our local vegetable vendor used his scrappy notebook and bits of paper for keeping track of payments, causing him frequent financial losses. He was also struggling to recover his dues and manage his inventory efficiently. I introduced him to Khatabook, a simple, reliable accounting app also available in Hindi, his native language. I installed the app which facilitated keeping track of his dues, even sending SMS reminders to regular customers stored flat number-wise. Inventory management section helped him track his bulk purchases and payments to the whole sellers. The most difficult part was adoption as he kept reverting back to his old scrap paper system. I had to send an hour everyday for a week till he finally started consistently using it. The SMS reminders was the one facility that had him hooked with his customers now remotely paying him via UPI which credited money directly to his bank account. A dedicated convert, he had much tighter control over his business now with near zero losses.

Helping a Grocery Store Reach Customers with Digital Marketing

The local grocery storekeeper had attempted sourcing organic grocery items from his native state Uttrakhand, however frequent unsold inventory restricted him from stocking it in bulk. There was adequate demand once the residents were made aware of the availabilty and health benefits of the items. Not only would sales of these organic products increase his profit, local farmers from remote villages of his state would also benefit financinally. To address this, I created Instagram and Facebook pages for these products, showcasing their health benefits and pricing. I also designed e-posters for them. I shared these on our society managemnt app and WhatsApp groups to spread awareness. Regular efforts increased traction,resulted in increased enquiries and boosting sales of those products. It exemplified how simple digital promotion can help local businesses thrive and expand their market.
